# Business Recommendations for Churn Reduction

## The Problem
- **26.6% of customers leave** each year
- **1,869 customers** churned
- **$1.66 Million** in annual revenue lost

---

## Three Things To Do Immediately

### 1. Fix Month-to-Month Contracts

**Why**: These customers churn at 42.7% - 15x higher than 2-year customers

**How**:
- Offer $5/month discount to switch to 1-year contract
- Send email campaign to 4,020 month-to-month customers
- Track who converts in 30 days

**Expected**: 30% fewer churns from this group

---

### 2. Kill Electronic Check Payments

**Why**: 45.3% of e-check users churn vs 16% for autopay

**How**:
- Offer $1/month discount for autopay signup
- Target 2,322 e-check users
- Make autopay the only option for new customers

**Expected**: Cut e-check churn in half

---

### 3. Focus on First 6 Months

**Why**: Churners leave 109% earlier than loyal customers

**How**:
- Welcome call within first week
- Check-in email at 30, 60, 90 days
- Priority support queue for new customers

**Expected**: Keep 25% more new customers

---

## Who To Target First

| Priority | Customer Type | Size | Current Churn |
|----------|--------------|------|---------------|
| 1 | Month-to-month + e-check + bill > $70 | ~800 | ~75% |
| 2 | Month-to-month only | 4,020 | 42.7% |
| 3 | E-check users | 2,322 | 45.3% |
| 4 | Tenure < 6 months | ~1,500 | ~40% |

---

## How To Measure Success

| Metric | Today | Goal (6 months) |
|--------|-------|-----------------|
| Overall churn rate | 26.6% | 18% |
| Month-to-month churn | 42.7% | 30% |
| E-check churn | 45.3% | 25% |
| Autopay customers | 40% | 60% |

---

## What This Is Worth

If you hit these goals:
- Keep ~500 more customers per year
- Save ~$450,000 in annual revenue
- Cost of discounts: ~$250,000
- **Net gain: ~$200,000+ per year**

Plus: happier customers = more referrals = free growth
